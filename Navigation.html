How to Navigate this Page 🛣️

The purpose of this site is to condense the amount of time needed to search up and organize different colleges and make it easier to figure out the factors that matter the most to you. This is how we organized this site to help you with your search:

  • Read through the home page! - the introducation helps to understand basic information and can help you decide what type of careers you want to focus into so you can figure out what types of degrees and what pathway is needed to get to your goal. From there, you can start to plan the college search as you have a much more clear strategy for what you are looking for in a college.
  • Location - is a very important factor in considering in which college you should go to. Luckily we have made that easier for you by organizing all four-year institutions into their respective states. All you have to do is use the Navigation bar to pick the state you want to explore and use the organizer to write down their locations and rank their closeness.
  • Factors - After condensing the list down to which colleges are viable for you based on location, you can then focus on the factors for each college. What major are you going for? Is tuition a big factor in deciding which college you go to? How about where you live or living costs? All of those factors we have put into the Navigation bar so you can easily click and figure out what you need to do!
  • Document - It is now time to organize and document all the factors of every college you are interested in going to. You can find out what each of those factors are such as the tuition, cost of living, or other things through the college's home page which is linked to every college within their states. Using the excel sheet below, you can document those factors as you explore this website and directly compare colleges to find the one the best suits you!
