Welcome to wonderful world of college! You have successfully gained your high school diploma, gained your associate's degree, or have made a big decision in your life to go to college. While this is an exciting time of your life, this is also a stressful part of life. Which college should you go to? How do you know which college is the right fit for you? Do you really have to search up everything about every college? While it is not possible to checkout every college in the United States, it is very important to learn about the different possibilities that you as a student could apply to and learn more about. Instead of wasting time searching up every college and trying to figure out the different factors to consider, we have made that daunting task easier for you by condensing all this information down into one website so you can easily compare and refer back to which colleges with just a few clicks. This can save you time to do other important things such as applying for college, learning more about financial aid, and considering other factors that you need to be aware of for college. With this site, we hope that it will be easier to find the colleges that you are looking for and figure out the different factors you need to consider when going to college!

All of those factors we have put into the Navigation bar so you can easily click and figure out what you need to do!
